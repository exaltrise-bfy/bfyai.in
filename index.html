FAQ

Questions.

Do you work with startups or established businesses?

Both. We tailor engagement depth and pace to stage — from proving a first workflow to hardening systems at scale.

How do you price your projects?

We scope fixed phases where clarity is high, and time-and-materials or retainer models when exploration is part of the value. Every proposal includes a clear milestone map.

Do we own the intellectual property?

Yes. Deliverables are structured so you own what we build for you, with sensible exceptions only where third-party licenses require otherwise — always disclosed upfront.

What is your typical timeline?

Discovery through first production slice is often weeks, not months, for focused tools. Larger platforms are phased so you get value continuously, not at the end of a black box.

Do you provide ongoing support?

We offer maintenance retainers, on-call coverage, and iterative improvement cycles so your software keeps pace with your business.
